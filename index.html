About VajraCore

At VajraCore Engineering Services, we embody the strength and indestructibility of the 'Vajra' in every project we undertake. Founded on a deep-rooted family legacy of engineering precision, we have evolved into a trusted partner for industrial infrastructure across Maharashtra. Our expertise spans from high-precision RCC core cutting to complex MEP installations and industrial chemical maintenance. We don't just provide services; we provide the technical backbone that keeps heavy industries running at peak performance. With a commitment to zero-compromise safety and advanced diamond-drilling technology, we tackle the most challenging industrial environments with surgical accuracy. Our mission is to bridge the gap between legacy engineering values and modern automation.
