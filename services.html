Our Services

A. RCC Core Cutting & Specialized Openings

We provide precise, vibration-free structural penetrations using advanced diamond-core technology. This is essential for maintaining structural integrity during MEP fit-outs.

Precision Drilling

Diameters from 25mm to 500mm for plumbing, electrical, and firefighting lines.

Structural Alterations

Clean horizontal and vertical cutting for slab openings and duct expansions.

Zero-Damage Tech

Minimal noise and zero impact on surrounding masonry or concrete.

B. Mechanical (MS & SS) Fabrication Works

Structural and architectural metalwork designed to meet heavy-duty construction specifications.

Structural Steel

Fabrication of MS industrial sheds, mezzanine floors, and support gantries.

SS Architectural Work

High-finish Stainless Steel fabrication for handrails, clean-room partitions, and specialized industrial fixtures.

Piping Supports

Custom-fabricated brackets and supports for HVAC and firefighting networks.

End-to-end HVAC contracting from technical design to final commissioning.

Installation

Installation of GI and MS ducting networks, including insulation and air balancing.

Industrial exhaust systems and basement ventilation for large-scale projects.

Chemical Floor Scrubbing
